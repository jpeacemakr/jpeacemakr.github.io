Command Blocks for Minecraft Bedrock Edition
Command Blocks

Downloads

Custom maps to show how command blocks can be used

Feel free to download and change these maps all you want. All maps listed here were created for Minecraft Bedrock Edition on Windows 10. For information on how to install Minecraft maps, click here.


Space station DOWNLOAD

This map features a space shuttle launch pad, NASA facility and space station. A teleporter can send you up to the space station. It demonstrates the teleportation and summon commands.


Coliseum of Death DOWNLOAD

This is a survival or deathmatch map that allows players to choose different characters with special abilities. Become a knight, wizard, super-fast lancer or summon a pack of wolves to fight by your side. This map demonstrates summoning, effects, enchantments and inventory commands.


UFO attack DOWNLOAD

A UFO circles overhead and shoots its death ray down at the ground as aliens hunt down humans. See how long you can survive. This map demonstrates the minecart command block and summoning.


Trapped in a tunnel DOWNLOAD

The players are trapped in one end of a tunnel as waves of zombies come at them. Use your weapons, TNT and other objects to hold them off. This map demonstrates block cloning and summoning.


Infinite minecart DOWNLOAD

This map uses a mine cart and rail that grows automatically and allows you to travel forever in either direction. This map demonstrates block cloning and how command blocks can be activated by rails.


TNT fight DOWNLOAD

Shoot the ceiling to rain down TNT upon your enemies. This map demonstrates block cloning and conditional command blocks.
